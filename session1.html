Introduction to Semiconductor Devices 1

BY - Dr. Sunidhi

This course serves as an introduction to the fundamental principles and operation of semiconductor devices, laying the groundwork for more advanced study in the field of electronics. Students will gain an understanding of semiconductor materials, basic device physics, and the characteristics and applications of various semiconductor devices.

    Course Objectives:
  1. 1. To provide a comprehensive understanding of semiconductor materials and their properties.
  2. 2. To introduce the basic principles of semiconductor device operation.
  3. 3. To familiarize students with the characteristics and applications of common semiconductor devices.
  4. 4. To develop problem-solving skills related to semiconductor device analysis and design.
